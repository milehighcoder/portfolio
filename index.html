about

Mychal Martinez is a Basketball Skills Trainer in Denver, CO with 8 years of entrepreneurial experience operating his own Limited Liability Company (Mychal Martinez Basketball). Mychal has worked with players at the highest levels of basketball including a Female McDonald's All-American and Male Divison I All-American in his time as a coach.

Due the impacts of the COVID-19 pandemic on his business, Mychal is seeking new career opportunities and is working towards acquiring a certification in Full Stack Web Development at the University of Denver's Coding Boot Camp. Graduation is set for March of 2021.

With an eye for design, an entrepreneurial mindset, and a coding skill set, Mychal feels he would be an invaluable asset to any established business and/or tech startup.
